## 🎨 Design Concept: "Spa Day vs. Sick Day"
The primary challenge of this project was organizing a wide range of services—from emergency medical care to high-end cosmetic fillers—without overwhelming the patient.

**The Solution:** An intuitive "Split-Hero" landing page that allows patients to self-identify their needs immediately:
* **The Spa Day Path:** Focuses on wellness, glow, and confidence (Botox, Fillers, IV Therapy).
* **The Sick Day Path:** Focuses on recovery, primary care, and long-term health (GLP-1 Weight Loss, Urgent Care, Lab Work).

## ✨ Key Features
* **Mobile-First Action Bar:** A sticky footer providing one-touch access to Call, Text, and Directions—essential for a local clinic.
* **Visual Hierarchy:** Clean "Service Cards" categorized by patient intent rather than a standard medical list.
* **Trust Indicators:** Highlighted medical credentials (FNP-C) to ensure safety and professional authority are established upfront.
